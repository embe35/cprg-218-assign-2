About Me

I have a passion for UX design and graphic design and enjoy creating clean designs that are both asethetic and useful. I have always loved creating traditional or digital art and my preferred mediums are acrylics and ink.

I am well-versed in the Adobe Creative Suite and in the big three coding languages. I am detail oriented, an artist, and have an eye for aesthetics.

I am currently working towards a diploma in UX Interactive Design. I also have an AA degree in Biblical Studies.
